# White House Cafe - Amravati ☕

A modern, premium, and highly interactive website for **White House Cafe**, located in Amravati. This project features a "walking-in" hero experience, smooth scroll reveals, and a warm, cozy aesthetic.

## 🌐 Live Demo
Experience the cafe atmosphere online:
👉 **[Visit White House Cafe](https://saranshaharwar-code.github.io/WHITE-HOUSE-CAFE/)**

## ✨ Key Features
- **Interactive Hero Animation**: A frame-by-frame walking sequence controlled by the user's mouse movement.
- **Premium Aesthetics**: A curated color palette of cream, coffee-brown, and warm lighting tones.
- **Order Online**: Integrated delivery platforms (Zomato & Swiggy) with floating micro-animations.
- **Live Maps Integration**: Real-time Google Maps integration for the Amravati location.
- **Responsive Design**: Fully optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop viewing.
- **Smooth Animations**: GSAP-inspired scroll reveals and staggered menu transitions.

## 🛠️ Built With
- **HTML5**: Semantic structure for SEO.
- **Vanilla CSS**: Custom design system with modern typography (Playfair Display & Plus Jakarta Sans).
- **Javascript**: Custom frame-preloading logic and intersection observers for reveals.

## 📸 Sections
1. **Hero**: Interactive "Walking into Cafe" experience.
2. **Ambience**: Showcase of cozy seating and warm lighting.
3. **Aesthetic Corners**: Highlight of the cafe's photo-friendly decor.
4. **Menu**: Digital card-style display of cafe offerings.
5. **Order**: Direct links for delivery and pickup.
6. **Gallery**: Grid view of the cafe's interior and exterior.
7. **Location**: Live map and contact details.
